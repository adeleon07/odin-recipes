Odin Recipies - Peanut Butter Cookie

Try this quick and easy Peanut Butter Cookie by Tamara B! Chewy deliciousness for all you peanut butter lovers out there! This simple, tasty recipe yields 12 to 16 cookies, but is so simple that it can be doubled (or tripled!) easily. It is suggested that you prepare the following ingredients below using the mise en place technique to make life much easier.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup semissweet chocolart chips (Optional)
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir egg, vanilla extract, and baking soda into peanut butter mixture; stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop mixture by small rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are flattened and golden, about 8 minutes.
